I am an undergraduate student at ZJU, majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics. I am also a member of Chu Kochen Honors College at ZJU. I would receive my Bachelor's Degree of Natural Sciences in June 2019.

Study and Research

I am interested in Geometry and Topology,Representation theory and Lie algerbas, Lie Groups, Partial Differential Equations and Analysis,

In my undergraduate study, I spent the first two years on equations and analysis, including partial differential equations, functional analysis and harmonic analysis. When I was a junior student, I visited MIT and took graduate level courses about Riemann geometry, geometry analysis, algebraic topology and Lie groups, which are all exciting things for me. This summer, I visited professor Naichung Conan Leung at The Institute of Mathematical Sciences at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, studied things about differential forms in algebraic topology and Morse theory. Besides, although my specialty is pure math, I also have some interests in applied math and computer science.

Recently, I am working with professor Wang Feng and Jiang Wenshuai about the differentiable sphere theorem and Gromov compactness theorem. And I am participating in a graduate level seminar about Lie algebra and a seminar about Riemann Surfaces. My bachelor thesis would focus on the differentiable sphere theorem.
